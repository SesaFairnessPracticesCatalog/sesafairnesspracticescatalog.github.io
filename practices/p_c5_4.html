Practice 4: Fairness validation strategies among different definitions and metrics
Practice description

C5: Model verification & validation.

Define fairness validation strategies and tools according to multiple fairness definitions and metrics (e.g. fairness probability measures or group similarity, or re-balancing strategies).

Application Example in Compas Scenario

Identify (according to specific metrics) a minimum level of fairness that the COMPAS module must respect for each group of individuals identified by skin color.

Addictional considerations

Adopt specific validation strategies to evaluate fairness levels of a ML-Intensive solution obtained 18 high responses in terms of positive impact on fairness levels of the system under validation. According to the experts, the practice requires an average or high level of effort to be applied (18 average and 22 high opinions), and it is averagely applied in practice (22 responses).
