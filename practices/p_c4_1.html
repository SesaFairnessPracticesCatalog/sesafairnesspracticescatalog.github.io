Practice 1: Fairness hyper-parameters tuning
Practice description

C4: Model training & testing.

Perform specific hyper-parameters tuning strategies to optimize fairness levels of prediction results (e.g. using specific global/local search strategies or adding fair-regularization terms).

Application Example in Compas Scenario

Apply global search hyper-parameters tuning to mitigate positive results among black and white people.
