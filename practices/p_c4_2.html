Practice 2: Post-processing transformation to balance results among minority groups
Practice description

C4: Model training & testing.

Use specific post-processing algorithms or strategies to obtain favourable outcomes for unprivileged groups (e.g. binary label transformations, score balancing between different sensitive groups, or community detection methods).

Application Example in Compas Scenario

Mitigate COMPAS model outcomes (using transformation techniques) among individuals with different skin colors but similar crime data.

Addictional considerations

Practitioners considered the application of post-processing transformations to improve fair results as averagely applicable (with 19 average opinions). Given that we also obtained 15 high responses in terms of frequency of application, we can think that this practice is more used when it is necessary to quickly improve the fairness levels of the ML-Intensive solution. Indeed, practitioners considered this practice extremely impacting in terms of the positive increase of the fairness levels of the solution under development (24 high or very high opinions and 13 average). In addition, we estimated a quite high level of required effort to apply this practice according to practitioners’ perspectives (16 high opinions and 12 average).
