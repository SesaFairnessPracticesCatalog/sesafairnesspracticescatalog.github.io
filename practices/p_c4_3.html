Practice 3: Post-processing strategies to optimize fairness levels of the system
Practice description

C4: Model training & testing.

Use specific post-processing algorithms (like matrix factorization or other recommendation strategies) to evaluate and optimize learning outcomes according to specific fairness metrics and constraints.

Application Example in Compas Scenario

Apply weighted matrix factorization in order to mitigate COMPAS model results among people of different ethnicities.

Addictional considerations

Similarly to the previous practice, applying post-processing optimization strategies on learning results requires an average to high level of effort to be applied (13 average and 14 high responses), but this one’s application frequency is said to be average (with 18 opinions). Practitioners suggested a high impact on fairness levels of a machine learning solution given by this practice (19 high or very high responses).
