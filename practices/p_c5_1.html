Practice 1: Validation strategies to detect discrimination according to different meanings of data
Practice description

C5: Model verification & validation.

Reason on socio-logical or linguistic meaning of data (or other information retrieval aspects) to detect the presence of discriminatory biases (using specific NLP, e.g. word embeddings or explainable ai algorithms).

Application Example in Compas Scenario

Create an approximate COMPAS model (with Explainable AI strategies) to identify which training features might be causing fairness problems.

Addictional considerations

Validating fairness requirement according to different meanings of data is considered a high- impacting practice in terms of positive improvement of fairness levels of an ML-Intensive solution (25 high or very high opinions and 17 average), that require a slightly high effort to be applied (22 high opinions, 18 average. A number of 22 practitioners classified this practice as average applied.
