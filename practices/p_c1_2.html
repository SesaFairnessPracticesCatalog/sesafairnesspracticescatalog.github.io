Practice 2: Multi-objective optimization for fairness constraints
Practice description

C1: Requirements elicitation & analysis.

Use multi-objective optimization approaches to balance different metrics and constraints.

Application Example in Compas Scenario

Satisfy multiple mathematical constraints in COMPAS problem formulation to simultaneously minimize dependencies between model outcomes and skin color, and increase the accuracy of the results as much as possible.

Addictional considerations

The HCPC algorithm results highlighted how the multi-objective optimization of fairness constraints in the formulation of the problem should positively impact the fairness levels of an ML-Intensive system; we collected 18 average responses, and 26 high and very high. Practitioners think that this practice should be used with average to high frequency, but the necessary effort for its application is high (29 preferences).
