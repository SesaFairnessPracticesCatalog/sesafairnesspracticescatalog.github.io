Practice 1: Empirical methodologies for fair requirements elicitation and analysis
Practice description

C1: Requirements elicitation & analysis.

Adopt empirical strategies (surveys, interviews, focus groups, cognitive walkthroughs, controlled experiments, or similar) to elicit and validate fairness-oriented requirements under specific fairness constraints.

Application Example in Compas Scenario

Interview legal experts to determine what fairness constraints are needed (e.g., features to base training on) to design the COMPAS system.
